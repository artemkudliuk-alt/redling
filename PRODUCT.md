# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Premium travelers, families with children, and business professionals seeking a peaceful, comfortable, and luxury-tier stay in Odessa near the sea, away from city noise.

## Product Purpose
Deliver an immersive, cinematic, and high-conversion landing page for REDLING HOTEL that showcases the hotel's premium rooms, pool, restaurant, atmosphere, and location, leading directly to room bookings or reservations.

## Brand Personality
Premium, elegant, serene, family-friendly, sophisticated, authentic.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS card grids and rounded boxes.
- Standard glassmorphism cards that block beautiful cinematic video loops.
- All-caps wide tracked kickers/eyebrows on every single section.
- Tiny rounded buttons and colored side-stripe card borders.

## Design Principles
1. **Cinematic Immersiveness**: Fullscreen video backdrops and scroll-linked video transitions carry the narrative. Design serves the video, not vice versa.
2. **Luxury Restraint**: No heavy background containers, cards, or borders. Text floats cleanly over dark-overlayed video backdrops using Montserrat typography and subtle drop-shadows.
3. **Strict Sharpness**: Strict geometric precision (zero border-radius on buttons, inputs, and elements).
4. **Seamless Continuity**: Seamless crossfades, double-exposure loader transitions, and zero-latency play-promise loops to avoid sudden visual cuts or black frames.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 on text elements via a custom 45% dark video overlay.
- Keyboard navigation and reduced-motion fallback options for transitions.
- High-resilience fail-safes (try-catch play logic, 12-second preloader escape hatches, 4-second scroll timeouts) for low-end mobile devices and spotty networks.
